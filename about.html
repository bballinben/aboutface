Our Mission

AboutFace empowers veterans and family members to turn their lives around for the better.

AboutFace The AboutFace-Veteran/Family Transition, Reintegration, and Community Education Program, Inc. (AboutFace-VFTRCEP, Inc.) is a 501(c)(3) charitable nonprofit, currently serving North Georgia communities. We are veteran and veteran family focused; offering personal and confidential attention, education, support and resources. We partner and collaborate with service providers and businesses to encourage involvement in our communityâ€™s veteran family safety net.

AboutFace also educates local businesses to help them better understand military culture and the need for their support and involvement. We encourage veteran employment, retention, and compassion for troubled veterans or veterans struggling as a result of their service to our country. The goal is to empower struggling veterans to turn their lives around by promoting purpose, healing, accountability, and the development of a sense of wellness and self-worth.

AboutFace is a proud member of the National Veterans Village Alliance (NVVA). The NVVA members agree to help each other in the development and sustainment of local veteran villages throughout the nation. AboutFace is initiating an effort to develop a local Veteran Family Health and Wealth Center.
